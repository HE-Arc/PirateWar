How to play

Getting started

First, create an account.

Then you can start a game. You have some resources to your disposition. With your wood you can buy your first pirate ship! Give it name and build it.

Quests (activities)

You can send your ship to some quest. They will be unavailable for some time and then will bring you some resources. You can choose a quest in the Select activity page. Then you choose a ship to send to that quest. And you wait until the ship return. The countdown is shown on home page.

Resources

Resources are the core of this game. The first type of resources : Gold, Iron and wood, can unlock the other type of resources : Crew, Cannons and ships.

So you can use

  • Gold to recruit crewman
  • Iron to build cannons
  • Wood to build, repair or upgrade ships

Ships

Once bought, the ships are editable. Choose the ship you want and click to go to edit page.

Then you can add cannons or crewman from your inventory to your ships. The more cannons you have on your ship, the more powerful your ship is.

You also can increment life of your ships of 1 point for 1 wood
